Overwatch is a 6 vs 6 fps game.
It first came out in late 2015 as a beta and not that much later it got launched on May 24 2016.
So this game is a 6 vs 6 shooter game, where the objective could be different every game.
You got:
  • Capture the point
  • Push the payload
  • Control the point
  • Hybrid between capture the point and push the payload
    •

With every game mode you've got different maps.
All those maps are different cities/coutries over the world.

The Overwatch League was created in 2018 and started with their first season.
The popularity really grew fast and so the OWL had to build their buildings bigger.
Many fans from all over the world now could watch the OWL live in the stadium or live on twitch.
At their peek there were like 20 000 people live in the stadium and about 40 000 online watching the grand finals from 2019.
